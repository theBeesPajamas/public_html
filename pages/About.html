About Me

Hello, my name is Alex(or Zander) Smith. I have always been fascinated with maps. I remember as a child staring at a giant world map in my bedroom, curious what the rest of the world was like. (especially the southern-hemisphere, since I was too short to really see much else!)I have fed my curiosity by studying geography, travelling , living abroad(New Zealand, one of the familiar and enchanting places on my childhood map), and now undertaking a masters degree in Cartography and GIS Development at UW-Madison.

I am currently based in Austin, TX where I work as a GIS Technician at Apple. Feel free to write me at smithaj8873@gmail.com if you have any questions about my maps, would like me to make a map for you, or just want to say hi.
